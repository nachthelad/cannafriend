# Cannafriend Handwritten Illustration Style

Use this style whenever generating Cannafriend cultivation visuals with ChatGPT image generation.

## Base Prompt

Create a warm handmade cannabis cultivation notebook illustration for Cannafriend.

Style: imperfect ink linework, soft watercolor fills, off-white paper texture, tiny field-notebook marks, practical and friendly without becoming childish or cartoonish.

Mood: calm, useful, lightly playful, like a grower keeping careful notes by hand.

Palette: nature greens, soft cream paper, muted amber highlights, small calm blue accents for water or app UI. Avoid neon, purple-blue gradients, glossy tech effects, and generic stock imagery.

Objects to reuse: cannabis seedlings, plant tags, grow light, watering can, potted plants, sticky notes, calendar marks, magnifying glass, phone with abstract app screens, journal pages, water droplets, nutrient bottle silhouettes.

Composition: clean landing-page asset with generous breathing room, readable at mobile sizes, no clutter, no hard shadows, no photorealism.

## Constraints

- No readable text inside the image unless explicitly requested.
- No brand logos besides Cannafriend assets supplied as references.
- No photorealistic cannabis photos.
- No neon lighting, smoke, party imagery, or dispensary/retail vibe.
- No generic stock-photo composition.
- No watermarks.
- Keep the cannabis cultivation context practical: care, tracking, observation, reminders.
